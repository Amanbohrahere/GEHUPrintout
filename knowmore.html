Welcome to Our Printout Service

Inspiration

Our inspiration comes from the high cost of printouts at Rs. 5 per page, which was too expensive for students to afford. We aim to make printouts more accessible by offering them at a reduced price of Rs. 3 per page.

Differentiation

What sets us apart is our commitment to affordability and convenience. With our service, students can get printouts at a lower cost of Rs. 3 per page and enjoy the convenience of on-campus delivery.

  • In-campus delivery: Printouts delivered directly to you inside the college campus.

  • Fast and reliable delivery

Target Audience
